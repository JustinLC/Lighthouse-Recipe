Stir Fried Ketchup Shrimps

Ingredients

These are the basic ingredients needed to make the dish. However you can add additional sauces and seasonings to suit your preferences.

Here is a checklist of the ingredients you will need:

  • Fresh Shrimp/Tiger Prawns
  • 2 tbsp Oil
  • 1 tbsp Minced Ginger
  • 1 tbsp Minced Garlic
  • 1 tbsp soy sauce
  • 3 tbsp Ketchup
  • 1/2 tbsp sugar

Instructions

You may check off the steps as you go:

  1. Rinse shrimp clean, trim antennae and pluck out the veins along shrimp's back
  2. Heat up wok over high heat, add oil, then ginger. Stir for a few seconds until you can smell the fragrance of ginger
  3. Add shrimp in and stir constantly over medium heat until the shrimp starts to turn orange
  4. Add in minced garlic and stir fry for 5 seconds or so until garlic fragrance arises
  5. Add ketchup, sugar and soy sauce immediately and stir fry until the sauce is dry enough to stay on the shrimp
  6. That is all. Enjoy it while its hot!
